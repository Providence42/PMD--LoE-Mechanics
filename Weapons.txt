Mechanics:
Base Mechanics:
	All weapons in the game have a base power value. This power value can be added or suptracted by the material 	used. Secondly, classes have unique weapons that only they can use. If other classesused them then they suffer a 	penalty of:
		ACC -= 50
		PWR /= 2
		EXP /= 2
	However, there are classless weapons that suffer no penalty.
